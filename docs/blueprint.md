# **App Name**: HealthSense AI

## Core Features:

- Patient Vitals Dashboard: Displays real-time vital signs and health data collected from smart medical devices, providing a quick overview of patient status.
- AI Diagnostic Assistant: An AI tool that provides preliminary diagnostic insights and potential risk factors by integrating and analyzing diverse patient data including imaging, lab results, and history.
- AI Radiology Report Analysis Tool: Utilizes Natural Language Processing (NLP) to interpret radiology reports, generate structured data, and highlight critical follow-up recommendations to prevent oversight.
- AI Communication Assistant: A generative AI tool that assists with doctor-patient communication by transcribing conversations, summarizing key points, and generating clinical notes or patient explanations.
- Health Risk Prediction Alerts: Provides proactive AI-driven alerts for patients identified at high risk of developing certain conditions or complications, enabling early intervention.
- Administrative Task Overview: Presents a dashboard view of automated healthcare administrative tasks, such as billing and prior authorizations, indicating their status and efficiency.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated rose pink (#CC6691) to convey compassion and innovation, ensuring good contrast against light backgrounds for key UI elements and calls to action.
- Background color: A subtle, very light pink-tinted white (#F6F0F2), almost imperceptible, ensuring a clean and soft 'white' canvas as requested, derived from the primary hue for harmony.
- Accent color: A vibrant magenta-purple (#B233A3) to provide an analogous yet distinct highlight for interactive elements and crucial information, complementing the primary pink without overwhelming.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif font, offering an elegant and high-end feel suitable for dashboard titles and critical highlights. Body text font: 'PT Sans', a modern humanist sans-serif for clear and comfortable readability of extensive medical information.
- Utilize a suite of clean, modern line icons for various medical functions and data points, ensuring intuitive navigation and quick comprehension.
- The dashboard will feature a clean, modular layout with distinct cards for different data streams and AI insights, optimizing for information density and readability, specifically tailored for 'pink and white' aesthetics.
- Subtle, fluid animations for data loading, chart updates, and state changes to enhance user experience and provide visual feedback without distraction.
